Tallinn, the capital of Estonia, is a captivating blend of medieval charm and modern innovation. Located on the Baltic Sea, this compact and walkable city is known for its well-preserved Old Town, vibrant culture, and digital-forward society. Whether you're a history lover, foodie, or tech enthusiast, Tallinn has something for everyone.
